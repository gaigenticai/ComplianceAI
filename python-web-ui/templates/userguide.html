Document Requirements

Accepted ID Documents
  • Passport
  • Driver's License
  • National ID Card
  • Government-issued ID
Selfie Requirements
  • Clear, well-lit photo
  • Face clearly visible
  • No sunglasses or hats
  • Neutral expression
File Specifications
Formats: PDF, JPG, PNG
Max Size: 10 MB per file
Resolution: Minimum 300 DPI
Quality: Clear, readable text
Tip: Ensure documents are well-lit and all text is clearly readable for best results.

How Processing Works

Document Upload

Your documents are securely uploaded and encrypted using bank-grade security.

OCR Processing

Advanced OCR technology extracts text and data from your ID document.

Face Recognition

AI compares your selfie with the photo on your ID document.

Watchlist Screening

Your information is checked against global watchlists and sanctions lists.

Data Integration

Additional data sources are consulted to verify your identity.

Quality Assurance

Final quality checks ensure accuracy and compliance with regulations.
